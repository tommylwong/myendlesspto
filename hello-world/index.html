About Me

September 22, 2022 /

Welcome to my little slice of the internet! I’m so glad you are here and I hope you find your own inspirations and reasons to see the world like I did.

A little bit about myself. I started traveling the globe as a digital nomad in 2021 when I temporarily had the opportunity to work remotely full-time. In early 2022, I got laid off in retaliation for refusing to go back to office in-person on a regular basis. I left my toxic 9-5 gladly and continued traveling the world without looking back. Since then, I’ve travelled to 9 countries, 16 cities, and counting.
